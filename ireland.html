Rosbeg

Rosbeg has a beautiful coastline, clear water, and stunning views. It is a Fishing and holiday hamlet. Life is focused around the sea and the great outdoors, with wonderful beaches, surfing, trekking, pony riding, and climbing. In 1993, maverick indie American film diretor, John Sayles, Shot "The Secret of Roan Inish" in Rosbeg. The story is based on the 1957 childrens novella, 'Secret of Ron Mor Skerry' by Rosalie K. Fry, originally set in Scotland.

Assaranca Waterfall

Assaranca Waterfall is one of Donegal's most beautiful waterfalls. The waterfall, named Eas a' Ranca in Irish, is set in idyllic surroundings close to Maghera Beach and is one of the real gems in County Donegal.

Bad Eddie

Bad Eddie or Eddie's boat are the names given to the old wreck at Magherclogher beach, Bunbeg Donegal. Cara Na Mara ('Friend of the Sea') is the offical title of the abandoned fishing vessel ran into trouble during stormy seas in the early 1970s.The boat featured in the 1985 "In a Lifetime" U2 and Clannad music video.

Slieve League

Slieve League or Slieve Liag is a mountain on the Atlantic coast of County Donegal,Ireland. At 601 metres, it has the second-highest sea cliffs in Ireland, and some of the highest sea cliffs in Europe.
