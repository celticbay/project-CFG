Disney's Art of Animation

Be surrounded in the artistry, enchantment and magic of Disney and Pixar movies. Explore the storybook landscapes seen in such classics as Finding Nemo, Cars, The Lion King and The Little Mermaid. From delightfully themed family suites to wondrously detailed courtyards, Disney’s Art of Animation “draws” you and your family in to become a part of some of your animated favorites.
